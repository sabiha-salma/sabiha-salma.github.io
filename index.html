About:

Sabiha Salma is pursuing her PhD in Computer Science (CS) from George Mason University (GMU), Virginia. She graduated with her B.Sc. in CS from the Department of Computer Science & Engineering of University of Dhaka, Bangladesh in 2014. Sabiha joined GMU in Fall 2018. Currently she is working in SAGE lab led by Dr. Kevin Moran, Assistant Professor at GMU. Her research is focused on Automated UI Analysis, HCI Considerations for Developer Tools, AI for Software Engineering.
